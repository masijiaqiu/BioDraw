violin_horizontal# "parameter-key"=default-value[optional-choice-if-available/data-type](description)
# All the parameters setting shold be string or float(int).
"title"=[string] (Set a title of the current axes.)
"xlabel"=[string] (Set the x axis label of the current axis.)
"ylabel"=[string] (Set the y axis label of the current axis.)
"figsize"="(6,6)" (Set the size of the figture, (x,y).)
"widths"=0.5[float](0.0 transparent through 1.0 opaque.)
"showmeans"=False[False|True](If True, will toggle rendering of the means.)
"showmedians"="True"[False|True](If True, will toggle rendering of the medians.)
"points"="5"[int](Defines the number of points to evaluate each of the gaussian kernel density estimations.)
"showextrema"="True"[True|False](If True, will toggle rendering of the extrema.)
